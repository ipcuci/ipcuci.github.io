Welcome to the IPC Student Chapter at UC Irvine!!!


The Institute of Printed Circuits is a Non-Profit Organization who connects current UCI students to Distinguished Companies Mentors such as Bose, Panasonic, and to lots of Startup Companies. IPC@UCI hosts monthly events for our club members!!!


This year, we attended the 2023 APEX EXPO at the San Diego Convention Center!!! APEX EXPO presents innovative technologies from a variety of companies in PCB manufacturing, computer vision, and defense. It's also a networking opprotunity with other Emerging Engineers and Industry Mentors!!!


There are a lot of benefits in building your resume, earning scholarships, and earning Technical Edu Certificates to kickstart your career. Each Academic Quarter, We Host One Technical Workshop, One Company Event, and Building an Electronics Kit/Holding a IPC Competition for Our Members.
